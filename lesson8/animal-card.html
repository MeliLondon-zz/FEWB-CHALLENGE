Black Panther

Whilst the animal is known as panther, it refers to 3 differents types of big cats: leopard, jaguar or cougar

  • Habitat: Dense forests
  • Conservation Status: Threatened
  • Diet: Carnivore (Medium-sized animals like monkeys, deers and rabbits.)
  • Life Span: 12-15 years

Black panthers are very good swimmers and very efficient at climbing trees. They are known to pounce on their preys from various heights. They also have very good hearing and eyesight, which make them excellent hunters.
